About Me

I am a graduating senior at the University of Washington, majoring in Applied and Computational Mathematical Sciences, with a specialization in Scientific Computing. I am particularly interested in researching applications of DL, statistical methods for solving problems traditionally approached through DL, and explainable ML. I am currently a Software Engineering Apprentice at Amazon MOD (Make on Demand), but plan to pursue a graduate degree in statistics and data science.


Outside of my professional/academic career, I am currently licensed as a USSF Regional Referee, Big 10 NCAA Soccer Referee, and Washington State Referee Mentor. I eventually plan to attain a USSF National Assistant Referee badge. Other hobbies include: calisthenics, piano, guitar, cooking, and learning Burmese Language!
